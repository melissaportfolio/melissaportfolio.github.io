Fishing leads to better health and lower blood pressure

Recent findings suggest that the pastime of fishing leads to lower stress levels and lower blood pressure. It's well known that fish are a healthy food containing omega-6 oils and have many other health benefits. Could it be possible that the time spent fishing is also giving you better health? Huckleberry Weather news spoke to local fishermen who laughed and said he wasn't surprised. Many people who go fishing like the pastime because it is so relaxing with the added benefit of a tasty surprise caught through their own talents. So take some time to head to a local fishing hole because it could be your ticket to a cleaner bill of health.
