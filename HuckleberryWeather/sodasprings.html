Hot springs are not only relaxing but good for you!

It's no surprise that the warm water of a hot spring makes you feel more relaxed, but did you know that there are more health benefits to spending time in a hot spring than relaxation? The water in hot springs are high in magnesium, which is important for the body. This mineral helps your body truly relax and function properly. We consume magnesium through the foods we eat, but your body can also absorb magnesium through the salt in water like what is found in a hot spring. You now have even more reasons to visit a hot spring. Relaxing and healthy!
